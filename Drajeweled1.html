Who is Jewel-embracing Dragon Fang, Drajeweled?

"The head of the Rainbow Demonic Dragons. Normally, it secludes itself in its own dungeon, the 'Rainbow Demonic Dragon Dungeon', protecting the Rainbow Magic Stones. "Rainbow Magic Stones" are made from the "miasma" that covers the dark region of the Dark States, and only the 'Rainbow Demonic Dragons' can crystallize it. In fact, it has the power to overpower the Demon Lords of the Dark States, but it basically has no interest in fighting, and when it is not crystallizing "Rainbow Magic Stones", it indulges in idle slumber within its lair. But if the magic stones that shine rainbow-colored were to ever be stolen, the world would come to know Drajeweled's terrible wrath."

Source

Deck Code: JU7U

Drajeweled's ability is to reduce the power of the vanguard, but with a very heavy cost of 4 cards with different grades in soul. Support cards in this deck either work with large amounts of soul (Quen Lu), or are units that aid in obtaining soul (Selfish Engraver). Engulf your opponent in darkness and swallow them with the power of Drajeweled and his minions!

Additional Cards not from D-TD02:

  • Selfish Engraver x3 (D-BT01)
  • Eye of Prohibition, Quen Lu x4 (D-BT03)
  • Lively Breath Dragon x4 (D-BT06)

    • Pros

  • The deck can potentially counter Overtriggers.
  • The deck has critical pressure and power reduction.
  • The deck can attack with high power columns.

    • Cons

  • The deck can brick and make it hard to achieve Drajeweled's skill.
  • The can deck out if games last long enough.
  • The deck is limited to a maximum of 3 attacks per turn.

    • Upgraded Drajweled Decklist

    Deck Code: KF8A

    Since D-BT07 has not been released in English, Drajeweled continues to make use of most of the Trial Deck given, adding a few cards such as Brainwash Swirler, making use of staple sentinels and trigger units, and a maxed out version of this deck will require a second trial deck. The deck plays out similarly to the budget deck, so it is advisable to wait for D-BT07 for more support.
