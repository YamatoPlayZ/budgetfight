Who is Diabolos "Violence", Bruce?

"Leader of Team “Diabolos”. A warmongering demon polished by hundreds of battles. His name resounds within Planet Cray even among mobsters from outside the Gallows Bowl. Brings his brothers (teammates) together with his overwhelming manliness and obstinacy."

Source

Deck Code: BVT2

"Final Rush" is Bruce's specialty. After the turn Bruce is placed, his "Final Rush" is activated, which activates a myriad of your vanguard or your rear-guard's skills. Unlike Nirvana, with just cheap support, Bruce gets a lot of tools to play with. The added commons are either better versions of cards in the Start Deck (Stephanie or Flinty Slasher), cards that help you stay on the game for your "Final Rush" (Special "Violence" Yell), or are cards that add other ways for the deck to win or gain advantage (Nate or Quen Lu). Make use of "Final Rush" to end the game in one fell swoop!

Additional Cards not from D-SD02:

  • Diabolos Charger, Nate x2 (D-BT03)
  • Special "Violence" Yell x3 (D-BT02)
  • Evil Eye of Prohibition, Quen Lu x2 (D-BT03)
  • Diabolos Girls, Stephanie x4 (D-Bt04)
  • Flinty Slasher x4 (D-BT01)

    • Pros

  • The deck has very strong turns once "Final Rush" is activated.
  • The deck attacks with high powered columns.
  • The deck is capable of unleashing waves of attacks once "Final Rush" is activated.

    • Cons

  • The deck does little to nothing until your 4th turn.
  • The deck can brick, or run out of steam if unlucky.
  • If the game runs longer than expected, the deck tends to run out.

    • Upgraded Bruce Decklist

    Deck Code: GKD6

    While the initial Bruce decklist above is decent, a maxed out Bruce deck does much more. With Diabolos "Unrivaled" Bruce, Bruce's evolved form added to the roster, the deck can dish out more attacks than its predecessor. Various support cards and stronger allies make the deck more consistent and win faster.
