Who is Youthberk "Protofall Arms"?

"A mysterious knight who raises the word "Skyfall" as he fights against the corruption and evil that infests the Keter Sanctuary both in the heavens and on the ground. The Skyfall Knight Order is not an organization recognized by the nation or the government, regarding the wealthy, who turn their backs on the poverty of the ground and line their own pockets, and the Cloud Knights as their enemy, but most of the dissatisfied people on the ground are their potential collaborators. It is said that wherever Youthberk appears, the cheers and support of the ground's people, and an even stronger force of resistance will arise."

Source

Deck Code: K3HE

Youthberk's gimmick is RevolDress, a mechanic that changes your vanguard into different forms. The trial deck gives you one form, RevolForm: Zest, which allows you to attack with high power and crit presure. With the power of RevolDress, become a great force of power that demolishes the battlefield!

Additional Cards not from D-TD03:

  • Divine Sister, Lepisto x4 (D-BT01)
  • Additional Angel x4 (D-BT02)
  • Daring Knight, Sauel x3 (D-BT06)

    • Pros

  • The deck can attack up to 4-6 times in one turn.
  • The deck has crit pressure defensive options.
  • The deck can attack with high power columns.

    • Cons

  • The deck is very reliant on your 4 copies of RevolForm: Zest.
  • The deck is not at full power without Persona Ride.
  • The deck cannot recover its field against removal decks.
