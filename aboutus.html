About Us

Matthew Bibaoco

My name is Matthew, I was born on January 8, 2005, in Bacolod. I was raised in Dumaguete for a short time, until I moved back to Bacolod. I am currently studying at Liceo de La Salle.

My relationship with Cardfight!! Vanguard started around 2015, when my neighbor and longtime friend introduced me to the game, which we soon fell in love with. Back then, we were not really aware of the concept of upgrading our decks and limited ourselves to Trial Decks as we thought they gave the most bang for our buck, as we did not have access to a lot of money. We soon realized that there were things we could do to upgrade our decks, so we started looking up cheap options, which we soon kept doing. With the accessibility of the game right now, with cheap starter decks and a lot of options of buying cards, Vanguard could never be more accessible than now.

This is why this website was made, in order to show potential new players how to play the game on a budget; limiting ourselves to trial decks and then upgrading decks with cards of the common rarity in booster sets, which shows them a basic version of the deck to see if they like it, and then showing a prospective upgraded deck for payers who like the archetype.
